FAQ

WE'VE GOT ANSWERS

How Long Does My VFX Project Take?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple motion graphics typically take 1-2 weeks, while complex VFX sequences can take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements.

Is there any video editing work you don't do?

+

We specialize in high-end commercial, cinematic, and branded content. While we focus on premium VFX and motion graphics, we don't typically handle basic social media edits or simple cuts. Our expertise is in complex, creative projects that require advanced visual effects and storytelling.

What programs do you work in?

+

We use industry-standard software including After Effects, Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Nuke, Blender, Cinema 4D, and Houdini. Our team stays current with the latest tools and techniques to deliver cutting-edge results for every project.

Are there any refunds if I don't like the service?

+

We work closely with clients throughout the process to ensure satisfaction. We offer unlimited revisions during the production phase and don't consider a project complete until you're 100% happy. If for any reason you're not satisfied, we'll work with you to find a solution.

Do you offer rush delivery options?

+

Yes, we can accommodate urgent projects with our rush delivery service. Rush projects typically incur a 50% premium and we'll need to assess feasibility based on our current workload and project complexity. Contact us to discuss your timeline needs.
